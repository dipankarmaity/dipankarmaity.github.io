I am a postdoctoral fellow at Georgia Institute of Technology under Pangiotis Tsiotras since Fall 2018. Prior to that, I received my PhD degree in Electrical Engineering at University of Maryland under the supervision of John S. Baras.

During my PhD, I worked on controller synthesis under logical and communication constraints and their applications to robotics. Currently, I am working on control and decision making under information constraints where we leverage tools from Information theory, optimization and bounded rationality.

I am broadly interested in controls, multi-agent-systems, robotics and Cyber-Physical-Systems.
